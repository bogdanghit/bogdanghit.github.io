Bogdan Ghit

I am a computer scientist and software engineer at Databricks where I build cloud computing infrastructure to make big data processing fast and simple. I received my PhD in Computer Science from Delft University of Technology where my research was focused on scheduling and resource management for big data processing frameworks.
